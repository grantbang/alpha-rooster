## Next Steps After Validation

**If ROAS >2x for 3+ months consecutively:**

1. **Form LLC** ($100-800)
   - Choose state (Delaware, Wyoming, or home state)
   - File Articles of Organization
   - Get EIN from IRS (free, 5 min)
   - Update affiliate networks with new entity info

2. **Open Business Bank Account**
   - Requires EIN and LLC formation docs
   - Separate business/personal finances

3. **Upgrade to Google Workspace** ($6/month)
   - Professional dedicated inbox
   - Team collaboration (if you hire)

4. **Consider Business Insurance**
   - General liability: $30-50/month
   - Protects LLC assets

5. **Get Professional Accounting**
   - Hire CPA for quarterly taxes
   - QuickBooks or Xero for bookkeeping

**Until then:** Keep it simple, keep it cheap, focus on validation.
