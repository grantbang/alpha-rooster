Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Play to Save, we may collect your first name, last name, and email address when you submit our form. We also collect standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, and referring URL for analytics and fraud prevention purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to connect you with licensed insurance partners who can provide you with free quotes. We may also use your information to improve our service and for internal analytics. We do not sell your personal information to unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information with our insurance advertising partners (including Champion Auto Insurance operated by MaxBounty) for the purpose of providing you with insurance quotes. These partners have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

4. Advertising Disclosure

Play to Save is an independent advertising service. We receive compensation when users submit their information through our site and are connected with insurance partners. This compensation may influence which offers we present, but does not affect the price you pay for any insurance product.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to track advertising effectiveness and improve our ads. This technology may collect information about your browsing activity. You can opt out of Facebook's data collection at facebook.com/settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Do Not Sell My Information (CCPA)

California residents have the right to opt out of the sale of their personal information. Play to Save does not sell personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To make a data deletion request, contact us at the email below.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at: privacy@playtosave.net
